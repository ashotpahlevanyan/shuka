Shuka · Consolidated procurement hub

One order, many suppliers, one container — from quote to escrow to a buyer's dock.

This is the RFQ-and-accept flow, not an auction: a buyer says what they need, Armenian suppliers submit real offers, the buyer approves a mix, money sits safely in escrow, and Shuka packs the whole basket into shared containers. Follow the money 🟠 and the goods 🔴 down the board, or click any stage.

The two hard parts live in stages 2 and 6. Stage 2 is the compliance agent that gates what may legally enter the EU/US. Stage 6 is the consolidation engine — bin-packing many suppliers' SKUs into shared containers and splitting freight & customs into a fair per-line landed cost. Escrow (stages 5–9) runs through a licensed provider such as Mangopay or Lemonway, not a home-built wallet. Money locks at approval, is held through fulfilment, and is released to each supplier only on verified delivery.
