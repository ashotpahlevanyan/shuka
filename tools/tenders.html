Outbound demand · Tenders & RFPs

Shuka finds the demand producers never see.

We scan EU & US procurement — supermarket private-label RFPs, public food tenders, HoReCa contracts, distributor calls — for anything the Armenian pool can fill, then bid as one collective. No producer could win these alone; the aggregator can.

Why a collective can bid where a producer can't. Tenders demand volume, reliability, compliance and single-counterparty accountability — exactly what aggregation provides. But every bid is gated: the compliance agent must clear all categories and the pool must confirm capacity before Shuka submits, because winning a tender you can't fulfil is worse than not bidding. Public tenders add bid bonds and delivery-penalty exposure. Opportunities shown are illustrative.
