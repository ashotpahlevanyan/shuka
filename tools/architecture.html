Read the three flows together and the model is complete. Demand flows in from the markets and is matched to producers; goods flow out as one consolidated container; money flows in as escrow and is released back to producers on delivery. Shuka sits in the middle as the single face of Armenian supply — the answer to producers losing the Russian market and having no way, alone, to reach the EU or US.
